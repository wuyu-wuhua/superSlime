Gameplay Guide

Basic Controls

  • Use WASD or Arrow keys to move your slime
  • Space bar to jump
  • Left mouse button to attack
  • Right mouse button to use special abilities

Game Objectives

  • Collect power-ups to grow your slime
  • Defeat other players in multiplayer battles
  • Complete challenges to unlock new abilities
  • Climb the leaderboard rankings

Advanced Tips

  • Use terrain to your advantage
  • Time your jumps carefully
  • Combine abilities for maximum effect
  • Watch out for power-up spawns

Multiplayer Strategies

  • Form alliances with other players
  • Use team tactics in group battles
  • Communicate effectively with teammates
  • Adapt your strategy based on opponents
